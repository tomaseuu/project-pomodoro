    Pomodoro Guide

    → Set a timer for 25 minutes to focus on a task.

    → When the timer ends, take a 5-minute break.

    → Repeat this cycle 3 more times.

    → After completing 4 cycles, take a longer 30-minute break.

    OR MAKE YOUR OWN RULES!!
